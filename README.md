# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 19, 2024

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **The Popular Gaming Newsletter** Discord bot ("the Bot", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. By using the Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with the Bot, we automatically collect:

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Discord User ID | Identify subscribers and track subscriptions | Until unsubscribed |
| Discord Server ID | Configure server settings and news channels | Until bot is removed |
| Channel IDs | Deliver news to configured channels | Until configuration is removed |

### 2.2 User-Provided Information

When you use specific commands, we collect:

| Command | Data Collected | Purpose |
|---------|---------------|---------|
| `/subscribe` | User ID, Game preferences | Deliver personalized news |
| `/setup` | Server ID, Mode preference | Configure news delivery |
| `/set-channel` | Channel ID, Game selection | Route news to correct channels |
| `/text-me` | Username, Server name, Game request | Process feature requests |

### 2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

We do **not** collect:
- Message content (except command inputs)
- Voice data
- Email addresses
- Real names or personal identifiers
- IP addresses
- Payment information
- Direct message content (except bot commands)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:
- Deliver gaming news to your subscribed channels or DMs
- Track which news articles have been sent to prevent duplicates
- Maintain your subscription preferences
- Improve the Bot's functionality
- Respond to game addition requests
- Generate anonymous usage statistics

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage

- Data is stored in a local SQLite database
- The database is hosted on secure private servers
- No data is shared with third-party services (except Discord API)

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement:
- Secure database access controls
- Regular security updates
- Minimal data collection principles
- No storage of sensitive Discord tokens

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Subscription data | Until you unsubscribe or request deletion |
| Server configuration | Until the bot is removed from the server |
| Sent news tracking | Indefinitely (to prevent duplicates) |
| Cooldown data | In-memory only, cleared on restart |

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data may only be disclosed:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety
- With your explicit consent

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

### 7.1 Access Your Data
- Use `/subscriptions` to view your current subscriptions
- Use `/my-stats` to view your personal statistics

### 7.2 Delete Your Data
- Use `/unsubscribe game:All Games` to remove all subscriptions
- Contact the developer to request complete data deletion
- Remove the bot from your server to delete server-specific data

### 7.3 Opt Out
- You can unsubscribe from all news at any time
- Server admins can remove the bot to stop all data collection

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age, in compliance with:
- Discord's Terms of Service
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 9. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with:

### 9.1 Discord
- Subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)
- We use Discord's API to deliver messages

### 9.2 Reddit
- News is sourced from public Reddit posts
- No user data is shared with Reddit
- Subject to [Reddit's Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy)

## 10. International Data

If you use the Bot from outside the country where our servers are located, your data may be transferred internationally. By using the Bot, you consent to this transfer.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy at any time
- Changes will be effective immediately upon posting
- Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of changes
- Major changes may be announced through official channels

## 12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your information:
- We will investigate promptly
- Affected users will be notified through Discord if possible
- We will take steps to prevent future breaches

## 13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:
- Use the `/text-me` command with your request
- Contact the bot developer directly on Discord

## 14. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
- Discord's Developer Terms of Service
- Discord's Bot Developer Policy
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
